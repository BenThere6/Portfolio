About Me
I began my studies at Brigham Young University-Idaho pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Software Engineering. I moved down to Utah and was unable to complete the degree online, so I changed course and got my Associate's Degree in General Studies.
While completing that degree I continued to work on personal projects that drove my desire and passion of coding. I built a couple in-terminal games, an auto homework-scheduler, and the most complex program I built was an auto budgeter, featured here.
Since then, I have received my certification in Full Stack Web Development from Rutger's Online Coding Bootcamp, where I worked on multiple personal challenges and group projects.
